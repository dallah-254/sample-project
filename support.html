Our Story

Pioneers in Automotive Lighting Innovation

Founded in 2015 by automotive enthusiasts, LightHub Customs started as a small garage project and grew into one of the most trusted names in aftermarket vehicle lighting. Our passion for perfect illumination and cutting-edge design drives us to create products that not only look exceptional but also enhance safety and performance.

Today, we serve thousands of customers worldwide, from weekend warriors to professional race teams, providing them with the highest quality LED lighting solutions. Every product we sell undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our exacting standards for durability, brightness, and reliability.
